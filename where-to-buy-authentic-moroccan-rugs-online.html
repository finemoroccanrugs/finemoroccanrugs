Understanding Moroccan Rugs

Moroccan rugs are a vibrant expression of Moroccan culture, intricately woven with the traditions of the Berber people. These rugs, including berber carpets and kilim rugs, showcase a rich palette of colors and patterns that reflect the landscape and heritage of Morocco. Unlike Persian rugs and Turkish rugs, which often feature intricate floral designs, Moroccan carpets typically highlight geometric shapes and zigzag motifs that are deeply rooted in Berber culture. The craftsmanship involved in creating these pieces makes them unique among oriental rugs and adds to their value, especially as more people seek to understand where to buy authentic Moroccan rugs online. Antique rugs and jute rugs also find their place in this diverse category, offering various textures and styles that cater to different aesthetics, from contemporary spaces to traditional homes. For a different kind of transformative power, a custom photo rug can bring a personal touch to any room.

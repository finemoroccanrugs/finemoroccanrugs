Maintenance Tips for Soft Textiles

Taking care of Moroccan Beni Ourain Rugs: Soft, Minimal & Timeless is essential to maintain their luxurious feel and aesthetic appeal. Regular vacuuming is key to keeping the soft fibers free from dust and dirt. It is advisable to use a vacuum with a gentle setting, avoiding beater bars that can snag the delicate fibers. Spot cleaning with a damp cloth can effectively address minor spills, ensuring that the unique patterns and colors of beni ourain rugs remain vibrant.

Proper storage is also important for preserving the quality of your moroccan beni rugs. If you need to store a beni ourain rug, roll it instead of folding it to prevent creases. Placing a cotton sheet over the rug can shield it from dust while allowing air circulation. Avoid placing your moroccan berber rugs in direct sunlight to prevent fading. By following these simple maintenance tips, you can enjoy the beauty and softness of your beni ourain rug for years to come.

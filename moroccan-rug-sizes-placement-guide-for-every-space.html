What are the common sizes of Moroccan rugs?

Common sizes of Moroccan rugs include small (2x3 feet), medium (5x8 feet), and large (8x10 feet or larger). If common sizes don't work, custom sizing is an option. The size you choose depends on the space and how you intend to use the rug.

How do I determine the ideal rug size for my living room?

For a living room, a good rule of thumb is to choose a rug that allows at least the front legs of your furniture to rest on it. Typically, this means opting for a rug that is at least 8x10 feet for a standard living room.

Can I use a Moroccan rug in an open concept space?

Yes, Moroccan rugs can enhance open concept spaces. Consider using a larger rug that defines the seating area and complements the overall decor of the connected spaces.

What should I consider when placing a rug in the bedroom?

In the bedroom, it’s best to choose a rug that extends beyond the edges of the bed. A common option is to place a 5x8 or 8x10 rug beneath the bed to create a cozy feel and add warmth.

Area rugs are ideal for smaller or defined spaces, while large rugs work well in larger, open areas. The choice depends on the room layout and desired aesthetic.
